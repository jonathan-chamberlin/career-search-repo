## VR / Immersive Tech

### Qualifications
Entry-level VR/Immersive Tech positions typically require a BS/MS in Computer Science, Engineering, or related technical fields, with GPA preferences around 3.0+. Candidates should be current students or recent graduates (within 1 year), and some roles require security clearance eligibility or prior internship experience.

### Requirements
These roles demand a strong CS foundation with coursework in computing and engineering fundamentals, plus demonstrated creativity and problem-solving abilities. Candidates must show genuine passion for interactive entertainment, 3D engine technology, and immersive experiences, with personal projects or a programming portfolio being highly valued.

### Skills
Core technical skills include C++, Python, and game engines like Unity and Unreal Engine, along with 3D graphics APIs (OpenGL, Vulkan, Direct3D). Additional valued skills include VR/AR development platforms, computer vision, motion capture experience, and Git version control, combined with strong communication and teamwork abilities.

## Machine Learning Engineer

### Qualifications
ML Engineer roles require a BS/MS (or PhD for advanced positions) in Computer Science, Engineering, or related fields, with graduation typically between Fall 2025 and Summer 2026. Competitive candidates often have awards from programming competitions (ACM/ICPC, NOI) and must have work authorization in their target country.

### Requirements
These positions demand strong algorithmic thinking, proficiency in data structures, and hands-on experience with HPC infrastructure and distributed computing. Candidates should demonstrate experience in NLP, computer vision, search/recommendation systems, or deep learning, along with the ability to optimize models for production at scale.

### Skills
Essential skills include Python, C++, and deep learning frameworks like PyTorch, TensorFlow, JAX, and DeepSpeed. Experience with GPU clusters, Kubernetes, Docker, and distributed training (DDP, FSDP) is highly valued, along with familiarity with LLMs, generative AI, and technologies like CUDA for performance optimization.

## Analytics / Data Engineer

### Qualifications
Data Engineering roles require a Bachelor's or Master's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or related STEM fields, typically with graduation by Summer 2026. Prior internship experience is strongly preferred, and candidates should be comfortable with hybrid or remote work arrangements depending on the company.

### Requirements
These positions involve architecting and deploying data models, building ETL pipelines, and performing data analysis for business insights across the full data lifecycle. Candidates must be comfortable working cross-functionally with Product Managers, Engineers, and Analysts in fast-paced environments with evolving priorities.

### Skills
Core skills include SQL (complex queries, joins, window functions), Python, and Java, along with expertise in data modeling, ETL processes, and big data technologies. Familiarity with cloud platforms (AWS, Azure, GCP), containerization (Docker, Kubernetes), and distributed systems like Spark is essential, plus strong data visualization and communication abilities.

---

## AI Engineer

### Qualifications
AI Engineer positions typically require a Bachelor's or Master's degree (or PhD for research roles) in Computer Science, Engineering, Statistics, or related quantitative fields. Candidates should have 0-1 years of experience for entry-level roles, with research publication experience valued for advanced positions.

### Requirements
These roles demand end-to-end ownership of AI-powered features, from user research through deployment, with the ability to move fast in loosely defined environments. Candidates should have product engineering experience building full-stack applications, integrating with APIs, and shipping high-quality products at scale.

### Skills
Key skills include Python, JavaScript/TypeScript, React, and frameworks like LangChain and OpenAI Functions for building AI applications. Experience with relational databases (Postgres/MySQL), containerization (Docker, Kubernetes), and CI/CD pipelines is important, along with understanding of ML algorithms, deep learning, and computer vision fundamentals.
