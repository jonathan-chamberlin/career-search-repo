# Skills Aggregated by Job Category

## VR / Immersive Tech

### Skills
Proficiency in C++ is essential across all roles, alongside Python and game engines like Unity and Unreal Engine. Knowledge of 3D graphics, physics, computer vision, and VR-specific SDKs (HTC Vive, AR/VR platforms) is highly valued, along with strong problem-solving and communication abilities.

### Requirements
Candidates should have a strong foundation in computer science with coursework in graphics, machine learning, or related areas. Roles emphasize building and debugging immersive applications, rapid prototyping, and collaborating closely with cross-functional teams including designers and engineers.

### Qualifications
A BS/MS/PhD in Computer Science, Engineering, or related field is expected, with many internships requiring current enrollment and a 3.0+ GPA. Some positions require security clearance or authorization to work in specific locations, and prior internship or portfolio experience is strongly preferred.

## Machine Learning Engineer

### Skills
Strong programming in Python and C++ is required, with familiarity in ML frameworks like PyTorch, DeepSpeed, and distributed training tools. Domain expertise spans NLP, computer vision, recommendation systems, and large-scale data processing, with an emphasis on algorithms, data structures, and effective collaboration.

### Requirements
Hands-on experience with HPC infrastructure, distributed systems, and accelerated computing (GPUs) is expected. Roles involve developing ML algorithms for production, optimizing models, and working on large-scale systems including search, ranking, and personalization.

### Qualifications
A BS or MS in Computer Science or related field is standard, with PhD preferred for research-heavy roles. Candidates should be current students or recent graduates (within one year), and must have work authorization in the target country.

---

## Analytics / Data Engineer

### Skills
SQL expertise is fundamental, including complex queries, joins, and window functions, alongside Python or Java for data processing. Familiarity with data modeling, ETL pipelines, big data tools, and cloud platforms is expected, as well as strong analytical thinking and cross-functional collaboration skills.

### Requirements
Roles involve designing and building reliable data pipelines, ensuring data quality, and creating dashboards for stakeholders across finance, sales, product, and engineering. Candidates should be comfortable working through the full data lifecycle from modeling to deployment.

### Qualifications
Candidates should be pursuing or have completed a degree in Computer Science, Data Engineering, Mathematics, or Statistics. Most internships require 12-week commitments with hybrid or onsite components, and coursework in algorithms, databases, and distributed systems is recommended.

---

## AI Engineer

### Skills
Full-stack development skills are valued, with proficiency in Python, JavaScript/TypeScript, and React for building user-facing AI products. Experience with databases (SQL/NoSQL), containerization (Docker, Kubernetes), CI/CD, and AI frameworks like LangChain is increasingly important.

### Requirements
Candidates should be capable of owning end-to-end feature development, integrating with APIs, and shipping high-quality products at scale. Research-oriented roles expect publishing experience and contributions to ML/AI communities, while product roles emphasize user focus and fast iteration.

### Qualifications
A Bachelor's or Master's degree in Computer Science or related field is required, with PhD necessary for research positions. Roles target new grads (Fall 2025 - Summer 2026) with 0-1 years of professional experience, and prior internship experience is a strong advantage.
