## Machine Learning Engineer

### Skills
Python and C++ are core languages, with deep expertise in PyTorch/TensorFlow for model development. Experience with distributed training (DDP, FSDP, DeepSpeed), MLOps, recommendation systems, and domain areas like NLP, computer vision, or autonomous systems is expected.

### Requirements
Strong algorithm and data structure foundations with hands-on experience deploying ML models to production at scale. Ability to work with large-scale data, optimize model performance, translate research into real-world applications, and collaborate across cross-functional teams.

### Qualifications
BS/MS in Computer Science, ML, or related field for most roles; PhD preferred for research-focused positions. Graduating in 2025-2026 with competitive programming awards (Kaggle, ICPC) or prior ML internship experience is advantageous.

---

## Analytics / Data Engineer

### Skills
SQL proficiency is fundamental (joins, aggregations, window functions), with Python or Java for pipeline development. Experience with cloud platforms (AWS, Azure, GCP), ETL processes, data modeling, and big data tools (Spark, distributed systems) is expected.

### Requirements
Design and implement data pipelines connecting various sources; ensure data quality, accessibility, and performance optimization. Collaborate with engineers, analysts, and product managers to deliver insights and maintain production data infrastructure.

### Qualifications
Currently pursuing or recently completed BS/MS in Computer Science, Engineering, or related STEM field. Prior internship experience and coursework in algorithms, data structures, and database systems are strongly preferred.

---

## AI Engineer

### Skills
Python is essential, with JavaScript/TypeScript for full-stack applications; familiarity with LLM APIs (OpenAI, Claude), LangChain, and RAG pipelines is increasingly important. Understanding of databases (SQL/NoSQL), containerization (Docker, Kubernetes), and CI/CD practices is expected.

### Requirements
Build and deploy AI-powered features end-to-end; integrate with APIs and services while maintaining production quality. Ability to work in fast-paced, loosely defined environments, talk to users to understand problems, and ship high-quality products quickly.

### Qualifications
BS/MS in Computer Science or related field for product engineering roles; PhD for research-focused positions with publications in major conferences. Track record of shipping products and previous internship experience is valued.

---

## Data Scientist (Consumer)

### Skills
SQL and Python/R fluency are required, with expertise in statistical analysis, A/B testing, and causal inference. Experience with data visualization tools (Tableau, Looker), experimentation platforms, and machine learning techniques (regression, classification) is expected.

### Requirements
Design and analyze experiments to support product decisions; develop predictive models for user behavior and engagement. Translate ambiguous problems into data-driven analyses and communicate complex findings to diverse stakeholders including executives.

### Qualifications
BS in quantitative field (CS, Math, Statistics, Economics) minimum; MS/PhD strongly preferred for senior roles. 0-5+ years experience depending on level, with strong analytical and communication skills.

---

## Data Scientist (ML)

### Skills
Python with PyTorch/TensorFlow is core, plus deep expertise in deep learning architectures (CNNs, RNNs, transformers, generative models). Experience with computer vision, NLP, neural rendering, or other specialized ML domains; CUDA and GPU optimization knowledge is valuable.

### Requirements
Conduct original research and publish papers in top-tier venues (NeurIPS, CVPR, ICML); translate cutting-edge research into production systems. Develop novel algorithms, collaborate with research and product teams, and mentor junior researchers.

### Qualifications
PhD in Computer Science, ML, Statistics, or related field is typically required; MS with significant research experience considered. Strong publication record and first-author papers at major conferences are highly valued.

---

## Analytics Engineer

### Skills
Advanced SQL is essential, with dbt (Core and Cloud) proficiency increasingly required; Python and Spark for complex transformations. Experience with modern data warehouses (Snowflake, BigQuery), data modeling, dimensional modeling, and Git version control is expected.

### Requirements
Build, test, and maintain data models for analytics accessibility; design metrics and dimensions that serve the entire organization. Partner with data scientists, analysts, and business stakeholders to optimize pipelines and ensure data quality.

### Qualifications
BS minimum with 2-6+ years experience in analytics engineering, data engineering, or related roles. Degree in CS, Math, Statistics, or Economics preferred; familiarity with dbt and modern data stack tools is often required.

---

## Data Engineer

### Skills
SQL and Python are foundational, with expertise in distributed processing frameworks (Spark, Hadoop ecosystem) and cloud platforms (AWS, GCP, Azure). Knowledge of ETL development, data modeling, streaming systems (Kafka), and workflow orchestration (Airflow) is essential.

### Requirements
Design, build, and maintain scalable data pipelines and infrastructure; ensure data quality, reliability, and governance. Collaborate with data scientists, analysts, and cross-functional teams to power analytics and ML workloads.

### Qualifications
BS in Computer Science, Engineering, or related field required; 1-3 years experience preferred for mid-level roles. Understanding of distributed systems, data warehousing concepts, and cloud services is expected.

---

## VR / Immersive Tech

### Skills
Proficiency in C++ is essential, with Python as a secondary language; expertise in game engines (Unity, Unreal Engine) and graphics APIs (OpenGL, Vulkan, Direct3D) is required. Knowledge of 3D graphics, computer vision, neural rendering, and VR/AR SDKs differentiates candidates.

### Requirements
Strong CS fundamentals with emphasis on 3D graphics, physics, and real-time systems; portfolio demonstrating personal projects in immersive technology is highly valued. Ability to rapidly prototype, push engine limits for quality, and collaborate with content/design teams on novel UX techniques.

### Qualifications
BS/MS in Computer Science, Engineering, or related field for entry-level; PhD required for research roles with publications in AR/VR/graphics venues. Prior internship experience and demonstrated passion for interactive entertainment and 3D technology are strongly preferred.
